Bangali Tuna Salad

Classically Bangali

  • Prep: 15 minutes
  • Cook: 60 minutes
  • Total: 1h 15m
  • Bangladeshi

Bangali Tuna Salad

Instructions

  1. Place the red onions in a small bowl and cover with apple cider vinegar. Set aside for 15 minutes, and then drain. ½ cup red onion,½ cup apple cider vinegar
  2. In a large bowl, add the drained tuna, pickled onions, cranberries, celery, capers, dill and parsley. 2 5 oz. can albacore tuna in water,½ cup dried cranberries,2 celery ribs,¼ cup capers,2 tbsp dill,1 tbsp parsley
  3. In a medium bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, lemon juice, mustard, salt and pepper (add the salt in 1/2 teaspoon increments, to taste). ¾ cup mayonnaise,1 large lemon,1 tbsp Dijon mustard,1 tsp Kosher salt,Freshly ground black pepper
  4. Add the mayo mixture to the tuna mixture, and using a large wooden spoon, stir the salad to fully incorporate all ingredients.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
  6. Serve with pasta, on greens, or on bread

Notes

  1. The dressing can be made up to 3 days in advance. You can assemble the salad up to 24 hours in advance. Give the salad a good stirring before serving.
  2. Leftovers will keep in the fridge for up to 5 to 7 days. It can be frozen for up to 2 months.
